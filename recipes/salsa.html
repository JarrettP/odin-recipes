Chipotle salsa roja

A quick and easy salsa roja made with canned Chipotle peppers in adobo.

Ingredients

  • 5 Tomatoes
  • 1 can chipotles
  • 1/2 Onion

Directions

  1. Char the tomatoes and onion under a broiler.
  2. Blend the ingredients to desired consistency.
  3. Finish with cilantro and lime.
