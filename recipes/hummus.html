Hummus

A classic chickpea hummus.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Chickpeas
  • 3 Tablespoons Tahini
  • 3 Tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 Lemon, juiced
  • 1 Teaspoon Cumin

Directions

  1. Process all ingredients in a food processor until smooth. Add water, if needed, for desired consistency.
